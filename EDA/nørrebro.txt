He has as of late been working tirelessly on an amalgamation of his various styles and modes of expression in his painting, and the result is this exhibition. 
The exhibition title can also be understood as a reference to the motives in the artworks themselves. Christian purposefully wants them to be ambiguous as to what the motive really is, and offers a plethora of alternative interpretations, each one as valid, strong and believable as the other.

Rest assured, Christian’s paintings are as surrealistic and expressive as ever, gritty and explosive, bristling with detail. The compositions are akin to an expanding chaos, yet upon close scrutiny recognizable shapes and familiar objects become crystalized and take over the narrative of the painting which opens for widely different interpretations.

For this exhibition we will present Christian Gundtoft’s most recent works, presented to the public for the very first time. On the opening night, we will also have a musical contribution from Linebug, an audio/visual collaboration between songwriter Line Bøgh and Christian Gundtoft himself.

Rumdesigner @idanemmingstudio udstiller på Flids forårsmarked med sin nye kollektion af håndfoldede lampeskærme. Lamperne er bygget op af to elementer; en klassisk foldet skærm og en guldring, der stabiliserer konstruktionen samtidig med, at den skaber spænding i kompositionerne. På trods af de kun to elementer er mulighederne for kombinationer nærmest uendelig, og det resulterer i, at der aldrig er to lamper, der er ens. 
Ida udarbejder sit håndlavede design på et lille værksted i København NV. Her arbejder hun endvidere med miniaturekunst samt -scenografi og hun eksperimenterer generelt med ting, der kan bygges af pap og papir.
